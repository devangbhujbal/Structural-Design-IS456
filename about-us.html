The Story

Why this site exists

Structural design calculations as per Indian Standard codes are scattered across textbooks, handwritten notes, and university materials — rarely presented in one clear, accessible place online. When students search for a step-by-step IS 456 design procedure, they usually land on PDFs, blurry scans, or content that skips the reasoning.

This site was built to fix that. Every topic follows the same structure: theory and code clauses first, then a step-by-step design procedure, then a fully solved numerical example. No skipping steps. No assumed knowledge. Every formula traced back to its IS 456 clause.

The content is written with both civil engineering students preparing for university exams and GATE, and practising structural engineers who want a quick, reliable reference, in mind.

This is a growing resource. New topics, advanced design cases, and worked example collections will be added over time. If you spot an error or want to suggest a topic, feel free to reach out.

References

Indian Standard codes used

IS 456 : 2000
Plain and Reinforced Concrete
Code of Practice (4th Revision). Primary reference for all RCC design on this site.
IS 875 : 1987
Code for Design Loads
Part 1 (Dead loads) and Part 2 (Imposed loads) used for load calculations.
IS 1786 : 2008
High Strength Deformed Steel Bars
Governs properties of Fe 415, Fe 500, Fe 500D, and Fe 550 reinforcement bars.
IS 1904 : 1986
Design and Construction of Foundations
Referenced for safe bearing capacity and foundation depth recommendations.
SP 16 : 1980
Design Aids for IS 456
Charts and tables for doubly reinforced beams, column interaction diagrams, and development lengths.
SP 34 : 1987
Handbook on Detailing
Reinforcement detailing guidelines for beams, slabs, columns, and footings.
