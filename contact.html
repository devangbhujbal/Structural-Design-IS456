FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you write in.

Is the content on this site free?
Yes — completely and permanently free. No sign-up, no subscription, no paywall. All design procedures and worked examples are openly accessible.
Which edition of IS 456 does this site follow?
All content follows IS 456 : 2000 (4th Revision), which is the current edition published by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). Always cross-check against the latest BIS publication.
I found a calculation error. How do I report it?
Please use the contact form above and select "Report an Error / Correction". Mention the page title, the specific step number, and the correct value. Errors are fixed promptly. You can also open a GitHub issue.
Can I use this content for my study notes or teaching?
Yes — for personal study and educational use, you are welcome to reference or print the content. Please do not republish it as your own or use it commercially without permission.
Will slender columns, retaining walls, or other topics be added?
Yes. Slender columns, combined footings, retaining walls, staircase design, and pre-stressed concrete are planned. Use the contact form to vote for which topic to add next.
Can I contribute a worked example or topic?
Absolutely. If you'd like to contribute a well-checked design example or a topic write-up, reach out via the form or open a pull request on the GitHub repository.
