About This Site

A reference built for Indian structural engineering

Finding clear, code-compliant structural design calculations in one place is harder than it should be. This site exists to change that — every procedure here follows Indian Standard codes published by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS).

Whether you are a civil engineering student preparing for university exams or GATE, or a practising structural engineer who wants a quick reference during design, this site gives you the full calculation procedure — not just formulae, but the reasoning behind every step.

All calculations use the Limit State Method (LSM) as required by IS 456:2000, with worked numerical examples for each member type.

Codes Covered
  • IS 456 : 2000 Plain & Reinforced Concrete
  • IS 875 : 1987 Code for Design Loads
  • IS 1786 : 2008 High Strength Deformed Bars
  • IS 13920 : 2016 Ductile Detailing for Seismic
  • SP 16 : 1980 Design Aids for IS 456
  • SP 34 : 1987 Handbook on Detailing
